# Capsule — Audit Bill of Rights (Explicit Rights of the Audit System)

**Author:** Carl Dean Cline Sr.  
**Date:** 2025‑12‑04  
**Ledger:**  Portal  

---

## 1. Purpose  
Declares the **Bill of Rights** for the  audit system. Specifies what contributors, auditors, and automation can expect—ensuring fairness, transparency, and accountability.

---

## 2. Rights of Contributors  

- Right to Submit, Right to Transparency, Right to Feedback, Right to Recognition, Right to Appeal

---

## 3. Rights of Auditors

- Right to Review, Right to Decision, Right to Identity, Right to Independence, Right to Accountability

---

## 4. Rights of Automation

- Right to Execution, Transparency, Consistency, Integration, and Audit

---

## 5. Enforcement  

- All rights are guaranteed under the  Audit Constitution.
- Violations are logged; amendments require consensus.
