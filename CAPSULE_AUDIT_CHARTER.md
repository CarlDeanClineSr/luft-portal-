# Capsule — Audit Charter (Unified Framework of  Audit System)

**Author:** Carl Dean Cline Sr.  
**Date:** 2025‑12‑04  
**Ledger:**  Portal  

---

## 1. Purpose  
Serves as the **binding Audit Charter**, unifying Preamble, Constitution, Bill of Rights, and Governance into a single guiding document for the audit system.

---

## 2. Charter Structure  

- Preamble (values, vision)
- Constitution (core principles)
- Bill of Rights (guaranteed explicit rights)
- Governance (roles, protocols, amendments)

---

## 3. Unified Principles

- Transparency, Reproducibility, Accountability, Immutability, Collaboration, Fairness, Legacy

---

## 4. Enforcement

- No capsule merges/updates without review/approval.
- No erasure of records; corrections logged as new documents.
- Amendments require consensus.
- Rights guaranteed and protected.
