## 1. Purpose  
This capsule provides **deeper statistical analysis** of audit activity, beyond summary counts. It quantifies distributions, correlations, and trends in parameters (χ_max, α, β) and review outcomes.

---

## 2. Statistical Metrics

- **Approval Rate Distribution:** Histogram of approvals vs. rejections across cycles.
- **Parameter Stability:** Standard deviation of χ_max, α, β across events.
- **Correlation Analysis:**
  - χ_max vs. dynamic pressure.
  - α vs. storm duration.
  - β vs. IMF Bz strength.
- **Trend Analysis:** Moving averages of parameters over time.
- **Outlier Detection:** Identify cycles where parameters deviate >2σ from mean.

---

## 3. Visualization Protocol

- Scatter plots with regression overlays.
- Histograms of approval/rejection counts.
- Line charts of parameter trends.
- Box plots for parameter ranges.
