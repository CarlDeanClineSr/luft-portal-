## Purpose
To document the transition from a classic data pipeline to the first *fully open, perpetually running, zero-bias solar-wind laboratory*.  
Preserve the shift in physics: one where observables are captured in real time, flagged for anomalies, and made auditable for all future science—ending the era of gate-kept, narrative-driven data.

## System Overview

- **Instruments Flowing:** ACE (L1, plasma/mag, >27 years), DSCOVR, GOES (X-ray, energetic particles), with hourly to 64s cadence.
- **Automation:** Every new proton, electron, Bz, temperature, and density packet is ingested, saved as audit capsules (JSON, md), fully versioned and mirrored in the LUFT portal.
- **No bottleneck:** Data and analysis are entirely open—no human scheduling, no grant filter, no narrative layer.
- **Silent Relay:** System runs hands-off; relay watches forever; anomalies are flagged, stored, and ready for prompt or slow audit.
